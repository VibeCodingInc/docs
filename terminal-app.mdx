## What you get

The terminal app wraps your coding environment with:

- **Presence sidebar**: See who's online without switching windows
- **DM notifications**: Get notified when someone messages you
- **Watch Me Code**: Go live and let others watch your session
- **Auto-record**: Sessions automatically save as unlisted recordings

## Requirements

- macOS 12 (Monterey) or later
- Apple Silicon or Intel Mac
- GitHub account (for authentication)
