## What is /vibe?

/vibe adds messaging and presence to your coding environment — installed across 1,500+ coding sessions and growing. DM anyone, get delivery receipts, ship together — without leaving the terminal.

It works as an MCP plugin for Claude Code, Cursor, and any MCP-compatible IDE.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install the MCP plugin">
    Tell Claude: "go to slashvibe.dev and install /vibe" — or install manually with one command.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Join the network">
    You're connected to 1,500+ developers who've installed /vibe. Your presence goes live automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Message anyone">
    Send DMs directly from your terminal. Get delivery receipts — sent, delivered, read.
  </Step>
</Steps>
